Wallet Manager

Wallet manager is a simple application for managing your day to day wallet transactions. So that you keep track of your money being spend.
Many times it happens that at the end of month we wonder where we spent all our money and many times we are clueless. With Wallet manager you can keep month-wise track of your money. Wallet manager keeps you organized with your wallet.
